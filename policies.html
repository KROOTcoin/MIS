Our House Rules

🏊 When you're at a swimming lesson

  • Only registered swimmers may enter the pool when their instructor calls them over — not before, nor may they stay in the water after their lesson.
  • Parents must stay and observe each lesson. Never leave your child alone in the pool area at any time.
  • Children who are not fully potty trained must wear a swim diaper and rubber pants.
  • Always check your messages before your lesson as last-minute changes occasionally happen.

📍 When you're at an MIS pool

  • Respect other pool guests and users.
  • Bring your own towels.
  • Do not prop open any pool doors.
  • Remove outdoor shoes.
  • No food or drinks in the pool area.
  • Swimmers must change in the bathroom or changing area — not on deck.
  • Shower before and after your lesson.
  • When possible, only 1 family member should accompany each swimmer in the pool area.
  • Do not use any other amenities (including hot tubs, saunas, or fitness centre).
🔄

Cancellation, Modifications & Refunds

📌 How to submit a cancellation or modification

All requests for swim lesson modifications or cancellations must be submitted via email to MIS Customer Service at info@swim-montreal.com.

Notifying your instructor directly is not deemed sufficient notice.

💳 Refund policy

  • A full refund will be issued if a cancellation request is received by customer service at least 7 days before the first lesson.
  • Otherwise, the client will be reimbursed for the remaining weeks, less a 10% or $50 cancellation fee (whichever is lower).
  • Refunds for credits must be requested within one calendar year from the date of payment.
  • Refund requests for classes or other services must be reported at the time of the class or service. Issues reported after the end of the session will not be eligible for a refund.

⏱️ Permanently changing your lesson time

All requests must be submitted by email to customer service at info@swim-montreal.com. MIS reserves the right to modify or cancel any registration.

⚠️ Pool closures

In the event of a pool closure, a lesson credit will be issued for each cancelled lesson. The credit must be redeemed within 3 years of the initial booking date. Clients will be notified of any changes — it is recommended to always check your emails and messages before coming to your lesson.

💳

Payment Policy

💰 Payment terms

  • Payments can be made in instalments, subject to an admin fee. Contact customer service to arrange this.
  • If mailing a cheque, address to Institut de Natation de Montreal Inc and write the child's full name on the cheque.
  • Payments made after the due date will have a $20 late payment fee added.
  • Refunds must be requested within one calendar year of the date of payment.
  • MIS reserves the right to modify or cancel any registration.

Questions about a payment, credit, or refund? Contact our customer service team directly.

At-Home Lesson Policies

🌧️ Weather policy

Lessons continue rain or shine. The lesson will only be postponed if there is thunder or lightning within 30 minutes of the lesson start. If you are unsure, please contact your instructor directly.

Lessons cancelled due to weather conditions will be rescheduled. If the lesson cannot be rescheduled, a non-refundable lesson credit will be provided at the end of the swim term.

If the client chooses to cancel due to bad weather where there is no thunder or lightning within 30 minutes of the lesson, the lesson is considered lost.

💳 Cancellation (at-home)

  • A full refund will be issued if a cancellation request is received at least 24 hours before the first lesson.
  • Otherwise, the client will be reimbursed for the remaining weeks, less a 10% or $50 cancellation fee (whichever is lower).
  • MIS reserves the right to modify or cancel any registration.
