More Than a Swim
School — a Mission

Institut de Natation de Montréal was built on one belief: that swimming is a life skill every person deserves, taught the right way, at every age and stage.

What Sets Us Apart

What makes us different

Three pillars that define every lesson, every swimmer, every season.

🎓

The #MISway Method

Our proprietary teaching approach introduces all swimming skills from the earliest age, developing each at the swimmer's own pace. Swimmers are never held back by a single weakness — we build on strengths and create genuine momentum. Individual skill evaluations replace blunt level groupings.

👥

Small Groups, Big Results

We keep our groups small by design. More personal attention means faster progress, higher safety standards, and a better experience for every swimmer. Our instructors know each child by name — and by their unique strengths and development goals.

📍

25+ Pools. Your Neighbourhood.

With over 25 pool locations across Montréal and the surrounding region — in hotels, community centres, and private facilities — there's almost certainly a MIS pool near you. We brought the school to the community, not the other way around.

Our Story

Built in Montréal,
for Montréal

Institut de Natation de Montréal was founded with a straightforward ambition: to build the most effective, accessible, and community-rooted swim school Québec had ever seen.

What started as a small operation in a handful of Montréal pools has grown into a network spanning 25+ locations, hundreds of certified instructors, and thousands of swimmers who have found their confidence, independence, and love of water through the #MISway.

We're not a franchise. We're not a recreation department checkbox. We're a team of swimmers and educators who believe that learning to swim — really learning, with technique, confidence, and joy — is one of the most valuable gifts a child or adult can receive.

Every season, every lesson, every report card is a step toward that mission.

Our Evolution

How we got here

From a single pool to Québec's #1 swim school — here's our journey.

🌱
The Beginning

MIS is founded in Montréal

Institut de Natation de Montréal launches with a small team, a handful of pool partnerships, and a bold vision: to teach swimming the right way — with individual attention, real technique, and no swimmers left behind.

🏊
Early Growth

The #MISway curriculum is formalised

Our proprietary teaching method is codified — introducing all skills early, evaluating individually, and building on each swimmer's strengths. Faster progression becomes the hallmark result families talk about.

📍
Expansion

10+ pools across Montréal

Strategic partnerships with hotels, community centres, and private facilities allow MIS to reach more neighbourhoods. The model proves: swimming instruction should come to the community, not ask families to travel far.

⭐
Recognition

Québec's #1 swim school

Word of mouth, 5-star reviews, and consistent results cement MIS as the most trusted swim school in the region. Thousands of families across Montréal now trust us with their children's swimming education every season.

🚀
Today · Spring 2026

25+ pools. Spring registration open.

MIS continues to grow — in locations, in instructors, and in the number of families whose lives are changed by learning to love the water. Spring 2026 registration is now open. We'd love to have you join us.

What We Believe

Our values

Every decision we make — from how we hire to how we teach — comes back to these principles.

🚀

Advance at every stage

No swimmer should be held back. The #MISway is designed to keep every person moving forward — regardless of age, starting point, or pace.

🎯

Strength-first teaching

We identify and build on what every swimmer does well. Confidence comes from success — and success breeds the motivation to tackle what's hard.

📊

Radical transparency

Every swimmer gets a detailed written report at the end of every session. Parents always know exactly where their child is and what comes next.

🌍

Community first

We plant pools in neighbourhoods — not the other way around. Being accessible, visible, and trusted in the communities we serve is central to everything we do.

🤝

People over programmes

Behind every swimmer is an instructor who knows them. We invest deeply in our instructors so they can invest deeply in every person they teach.

💙

Swimming for life

This isn't just a recreational activity. Swimming is a safety skill, a life skill, and a gift. We take that responsibility seriously at every level of our school.
