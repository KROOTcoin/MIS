Our Approach

The #MISway — your teaching toolkit

You won't just follow a script. You'll master a curriculum built to get real results for every swimmer you coach.

📖 The Approach

MIS introduces all swimming skills at a young age, developing each at the swimmer's own pace. Skills are evaluated independently — not by levels — so every child builds on strengths while addressing weaknesses in a tailored curriculum.

✅ Instructor Benefits

  • Swimmers advance faster — you see real progress every session
  • No swimmer is held back by one weakness, making your job more rewarding
  • Clear evaluation tools so you always know what to work on
  • Advanced swimmers can be recommended for the MIS Swim Team

📊 Evaluations

At the end of every session, each swimmer gets a detailed report card completed by their coach. This transparency builds parent trust and keeps your work visible and valued.

The Position

Is it you we're looking for?

Here's exactly what the swim instructor role looks like day-to-day — and what we do to set you up for success.

🏊

What You'll Do

  • Deliver high-quality swimming lessons to children and adults using the #MISway curriculum
  • Manage and communicate with clients before and after lessons
  • Schedule and coordinate make-up lessons as needed
  • Respond to swimmer questions and concerns with professionalism and warmth
  • Complete detailed report cards at the end of each session
  • Represent MIS values with energy, patience and enthusiasm
🚀

How We Set You Up

  • Personalized onboarding and training in the #MISway teaching approach
  • Ongoing support from a qualified and responsive administrative team
  • Digital scheduling and management tools so logistics stay simple
  • Promotional resources to help build your roster of swimmers
  • Regular feedback and check-ins to help you grow as an instructor
  • A community of experienced peers you can learn from
📋
Qualifications
A valid lifeguard or swim instructor certification is required (NLS, RLSS, or equivalent). We welcome all experience levels — if you have a passion for swimming and people, we want to hear from you.
