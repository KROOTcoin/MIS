What We Do

Your lifeguard handles everything pool-side

You're a host, not a lifeguard. Our certified MIS lifeguards take full responsibility for the pool area so you can be present with your guests — relaxed, confident, and in the moment.

Your lifeguard will:

  • Identify hazards and enforce pool rules kindly but firmly
  • Constantly scan the water and surrounding pool area
  • Take preventative action before problems escalate
  • Be ready to jump to the rescue at any moment
  • Work with you to flag any areas needing extra attention
  • Arrive early, dressed and fully prepared to begin

The MIS Process

What to expect on the day

Here's exactly what happens from the moment your lifeguard arrives to the last swimmer leaving the pool.

Before the Event

1
Your lifeguard arrives early and performs a visual inspection of the pool to identify any hazards and ensure the area is safe before anyone enters the water.
2
They arrive dressed and ready to go — no delays, no setup time. Just immediate, professional pool-side presence.
3
They'll walk through any pool-specific rules or concerns with you as host, so everyone is aligned before the fun begins.

During the Event

4
Constant scanning of the water and surrounding pool area — your lifeguard never takes their eyes off the swimmers.
5
Hazard identification and preventative action — our lifeguards are trained to spot and address risks before they become incidents.
6
Pool rules are enforced kindly but firmly — your lifeguard is approachable and friendly, not intimidating. Safety with a smile.
