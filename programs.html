Confidence —
Parent & Baby Classes

Our youngest swimmers begin their aquatic journey alongside a parent or caregiver. These gentle, joyful classes are designed to build genuine water comfort — not just splashing around, but real, lasting confidence from the very earliest age.

The #MISway introduces all swimming skills from the start, allowing each child to develop at their own natural pace with professional guidance.

  • Parent/caregiver in the water with baby at all times
  • Structured around your child's developmental stage
  • Builds breath control, submersion comfort, and floating skills
  • Detailed progress report at the end of every session
  • Available at 25+ pool locations across Montréal

Strong Swimmer —
Technique & Mastery

For children who are ready to go further. This program focuses on refining all four strokes, building endurance, and developing the technical skills that turn capable swimmers into truly strong ones.

Swimmers in this program are evaluated on individual skills — not grouped by level — so every child progresses exactly where they are strongest.

  • All four strokes: freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, butterfly
  • Flip turns, dives, and competitive start techniques
  • Endurance and distance training introduced progressively
  • Individual skill evaluations — not level-based
  • Pathway to the MIS Swim Team for top performers

Learning to swim as a teen or adult is one of the most empowering things a person can do. Our instructors specialise in adult learners — patient, professional, and trained to work with anxiety, previous bad experiences, or simply starting from zero.

Our Method

What makes #MISway different?

Every program is built on the same foundational teaching philosophy — one that produces faster results and more confident swimmers at every age.

1

All skills introduced early

Rather than waiting for "readiness," MIS introduces all swimming skills from the beginning — allowing each swimmer to develop them at their natural pace.

2

Strength-based evaluation

Swimmers are never held back by weaknesses. We identify and build on strengths first, creating genuine momentum and self-belief.

3

Individual skill tracking

No blanket levels. Each swimmer is evaluated on specific, discrete skills — giving a precise picture of exactly where they are and what's next.

4

Full session report cards

At the end of every session, every swimmer receives a detailed written report from their coach — complete transparency for parents and swimmers alike.
