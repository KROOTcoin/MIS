📋 Registration

Registration is ongoing throughout the year, however it is subject to program availability.

MIS offers 4 terms per year — Fall, Winter, Spring, and Summer. Registration start dates are posted at the top of our programs page. Current term dates:

  • Fall Term: September to December (15–16 weeks) — register from August
  • Winter Term: January to March (9–10 weeks) — register from December
  • Spring Term: March to June (15–16 weeks) — register from February
  • Summer Term: June to August (9–10 weeks) — register from May

Spring 2026 registration is currently open now. Register here →

Our programs are designed to be accessible for all ages and abilities. Here's a quick guide:

  • Confidence (ages 3–30 months): Parent-and-baby classes. Parent gets in the pool.
  • Independence (ages 2+): Solo lessons for children new to swimming without a parent.
  • Strong Swimmer (ages 5+): Technique and endurance — all four strokes.
  • Swim for Life (ages 12+): Teens and adults at any level, including complete beginners.

If you're still unsure, contact us and we'll help you find the right fit based on your child's age, experience, and comfort in the water.

🏊 Lessons & Classes

Parents are required to participate from within the pool for our Confidence level programs, which include:

  • Parent & Baby
  • Parent & Tot

Once swimmers start our Independence level programs — private swimming lessons and group swimming lessons — parents are no longer required or encouraged to be in the pool. You're welcome to watch from the poolside.

If your instructor hasn't arrived within 10 minutes of your scheduled lesson start time, please contact them directly via their contact details provided during registration.

If you cannot reach your instructor, please contact our admin team at info@swim-montreal.com or call (514) 294-8259 and we'll resolve the situation immediately.

Please do not wait more than 15 minutes without making contact — we want to make sure every lesson happens as scheduled.

Your instructor's contact details are shared with you during the registration and onboarding process. You can reach them directly for scheduling questions, make-up lesson requests, or lesson-specific queries.

For general enquiries, registration changes, or anything administrative, please contact our team at info@swim-montreal.com or call (514) 294-8259.

If you need to miss a lesson, please contact your instructor as early as possible to let them know.

Depending on the notice given and the circumstances, a make-up lesson may be arranged or a credit issued in accordance with our cancellation policy. Please review our full Policies page for details on what qualifies for a credit.

Credits are applied to your account and can be used toward future lessons.

📄 Policies & Credits

Credits for eligible cancelled swimming lessons are calculated and applied to your account within a reasonable timeframe following the cancellation.

Credits are issued based on the applicable cancellation policy at the time of the cancellation. Please review our Policies page for the full breakdown of what qualifies for a credit and under what conditions.

If you believe you are owed a credit that hasn't been applied, please contact us at info@swim-montreal.com.

Lesson credits are applied to your MIS account and can be used toward future lesson bookings or renewals.

When registering for your next session or booking a make-up lesson, your instructor or our admin team can apply available credits to your balance. Please contact us if you need assistance applying your credits.

Our cancellation policy outlines the conditions under which lessons may be cancelled and what compensation (credit or make-up lesson) applies. Key points:

  • Cancellations with sufficient advance notice may qualify for a credit or make-up lesson.
  • Last-minute cancellations without adequate notice may result in a forfeited lesson.
  • Instructor-initiated cancellations will always result in a credit or make-up lesson.

Please visit our full Policies page for complete details, including specific notice periods and special circumstances.

📍 Pools & Locations

Our partner pools maintain a water temperature between 80°F and 85°F (27°C–29°C).

This range provides a comfortable environment for swimmers of all ages while supporting effective learning and technique development. This is particularly well-suited for our youngest swimmers in the Confidence programs.

This varies by location. Some of our pool partner locations have dedicated lane time or areas reserved exclusively for MIS lessons, while others may share the pool with other users or activities.

Your instructor will always ensure a safe and focused lesson environment regardless of the pool setup. If you have concerns about a specific location, please contact us and we can provide details for your pool.

🏡 At-Home Lessons

Lessons are always on, rain or shine. The only exception is if there is thunder or lightning within 30 minutes of the start of the lesson, at which point your instructor will confirm if the lesson is cancelled.

Any lesson cancelled due to thunder or lightning will either be made up or credited to your account.

If the client chooses to cancel the lesson due to bad weather where there is no thunder or lightning within 30 minutes of the start of the lesson, then the lesson is considered lost and no credit is issued.

At-home lessons are available for swimmers with access to a private or apartment pool. One of our certified instructors comes directly to your location and delivers a fully personalized lesson in your own pool.

Every lesson is adapted to the individual swimmer. There is no better way to learn than in a familiar, comfortable environment with one-on-one attention.

At-home lessons are available across Montréal and surrounding areas. Contact us to arrange your first session.
